About Wendy

Hi, I'm Wendy — and I'm passionate about people.

I'm a licensed social worker with 16 years of experience and over a decade as an HR leader and executive. I combine strategic know-how with compassion, helping people and organizations thrive — especially in male-dominated industries.

A proud mom of three, I've faced life's challenges with resilience and find joy in a supportive partner, self-help books, camping, boating, traveling, and soaking up the summer sun. All of it fuels my positive outlook and my work with you.

I specialize in leadership development, diversity and inclusion, team building, and organizational transformation — blending evidence-based HR practices with psychological insights to create real, lasting change.
